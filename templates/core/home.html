The Science of Smoke

Understanding the percolation and filtration technology behind our luxury glass bongs.

Borosilicate Purity

Unlike standard soft glass, our heavy-wall borosilicate (commonly known by the trademark Pyrex) offers incredible thermal shock resistance. This means you can use ice pinches and hot water interchangeably without fear of stress fractures.

Advanced Percolation

Our inline, matrix, and honeycomb percolators are designed to maximize surface area. By breaking the smoke into thousands of micro-bubbles, water filtration cools the vapor instantly, ensuring a remarkably smooth, terpene-rich draw every time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium glass pieces.

American borosilicate glass (often referred to as Schott Duran or Pyrex) is highly resistant to thermal shock. This means it won't crack or shatter under extreme temperature changes. Our pieces feature thick walls (5mm to 7mm) and are annealed in computerized kilns to relieve structural stress, ensuring maximum durability and lifetime longevity.

Every genuine piece hand-blown by our artisans is stamped with a subtle, sandblasted holographic brand seal and comes with a signed Certificate of Authenticity. Each certificate includes a unique registration number which you can link directly to your customer account profile dashboard.

We package all items in dual-walled boxes wrapped in thick protective layers of custom foam. We offer 100% insured shipping worldwide. In the highly unlikely event that your piece arrives damaged, simply take a photo of the item and the packaging within 24 hours of delivery and send it to our support team; we will ship a replacement immediately at no cost.
