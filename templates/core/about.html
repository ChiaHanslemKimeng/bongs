OUR STORY

Crafting the future of luxury glass bongs through art, science, and obsession.

Our Mission

Redefining What a Bong Can Be

Founded by glass enthusiasts and industrial designers, BONGS emerged from a single belief: the ritual of smoking deserves the same reverence as fine dining or premium spirits. We set out to create pieces that are as breathtaking to look at as they are to use.

The Craft

Born in Fire,
Perfected by Hand

Every piece begins as a molten mass of borosilicate glass heated to over 2,000°F. Our master lampworkers — led by Elias Vance, a 15-year veteran of the Pacific Northwest glass scene — shape each bong, rig, and bubbler by hand. There are no molds. No shortcuts. Only heat, breath, and years of practiced skill.

Elias is joined by a carefully curated team of specialists: joint welders who ensure every connection is airtight and perfectly angled, percolator technicians who design and test each filtration system for optimal drag, and finishing artists who polish each piece to a flawless clarity.

Material Science

The Gold Standard:
American Borosilicate

We source only the highest-grade, American-made borosilicate glass. Originally developed for laboratory glassware due to its exceptional thermal resistance, borosilicate's low coefficient of thermal expansion means it can withstand rapid temperature changes without cracking — an essential property for a bong that gets used daily.

Every piece is subjected to a rigorous 12-point quality control inspection before it leaves our studio. We check for micro-fractures, joint integrity, percolator flow rates, and cosmetic clarity. If a piece doesn't meet our standard, it doesn't ship. Simple as that.

The Founders

A Vision for the Future of Smoking

We felt the market lacked a true luxury bong brand that didn't compromise on function. We spent two years in R&D — studying fluid dynamics, sourcing borosilicate suppliers, and collaborating with independent glass artists — before releasing our first collection.

Our mission is simple: create glass that you are proud to display as art in your living space, that performs flawlessly every single session, and that lasts a lifetime if properly cared for.

Planet First

Luxury Without the Footprint

Our studio runs on 100% renewable energy. All packaging is plastic-free, using FSC-certified card stock and natural wood wool padding. Our glass is sourced domestically to minimize shipping emissions. Luxury, in our world, has to be sustainable.
