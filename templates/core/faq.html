American borosilicate glass (often referred to as Schott Duran or Pyrex) is highly resistant to thermal shock. This means it won't crack or shatter under extreme temperature changes. Our pieces feature thick walls (5mm to 7mm) and are annealed in computerized kilns to relieve structural stress, ensuring maximum durability and lifetime longevity.

Most of our standard beaker bongs and straight tubes come with a 14mm female joint and include a 14mm matching bowl. Some larger pieces and custom dab rigs may feature 18mm or 10mm joints respectively. Please check the product description for specific details on each piece.
